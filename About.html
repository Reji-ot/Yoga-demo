Our Experts

Each of our experts has dwelled in the world of wellness and carries a plethora of experience that spans across 2 decades. We are thus certain that what we offer here is not the quotidian programs that horde the internet and at every possible juncture.

Guru Dheeraj

A maven in Yogic practice and principles. Dheeraj ji was born in Rishikesh- India (Global Capital of Yoga) and thus his bond with Yoga was innate in many ways. This however, didn’t limit him from acquiring professional coaching in disparate health program. He completed Masters in Yoga (India, 2013), Certification in Romana Pilates (Mauritius, 2013), Diploma in Yogic Science (India, 2006) and 200 Hours of Yoga.

He has participated in numerous seminars that highlighted the significance of Yoga, Psychotherapy and spirituality in modern aeon. He never shied away from edifying others with the cognizance he possessed. In the initial days, he joined as a Yoga instructor with a school in his neighbourhood but soon travelled across Asia and continued in his teaching journey. He worked in Jendya Yogic Centre, Zhengzhou- China (2007-2009), Shanti Maurice & Four Seasons, Mauritius & Chiang Mai (2009-2021) and in present day he is associated with ‘My Wellness services’. We are as much as pleased as you to have Dheerajji join and willing to guide our premium members, in performing yoga in its authentic form.

Dr Raja

A stalwart in the space of alternate medicine, healthcare and fitness. He hails from a traditional Tamil family, where the mothers make use of various spices, herbs and medicinal leaves to cure ailments, eg: cold, fever, headache etc. Growing up, he learnt from his parents about the innumerable herbs and spices used in their household and their affects on our health.

His early interaction with alternative medicine prompted him to look beyond modern medicine for cure. He completed Bachelor’s degree in Naturopathy & Yogic Science (BNYS) from T.N Medical College of Naturopathy and Yogic Science. He went on to hone his skills in several alternative therapies like Mud therapy, Hydro therapy, Fasting, Iridology, Diet therapy and Counselling. He also possesses a deep understanding and practices Asian therapies: Acupuncture & acupressure, Reflexology, Shiatsu, Chi nei tsang & Toksen. He is well versed with conducting various wellness program, diet counselling and nutrition. Over the years, he has been part of many health programs that focuses on alternative healing therapies. At ‘My Wellness Services’, we are delighted to have Dr. Raja as one of our experts, who shall enlighten our members with regards to nutrition, diet, natural healing and alternate therapies through one of the many courses/ counselling available with us.
